About

a

Hi, I am Kris. A PhD student interested in research about standardization of FinTech technology and quant finance with ML and NN.

"I studied my second master of Financial Technology at the University of Glasgow after leaving investment banking work across China and the U.S. With a positivist, data-driven orientation, I specialize in empirical and quantitative analysis in my preivous work, bring the structured and efficiency focus to real-world and my life.

However, I am currently extending my work into qualitative research on standardisation in the FinTech industry. Theories and conceptual frameworks enable me to interpret how science and technology evolve over time, including the trajectories through which innovations diffuse and mature. Examining systems and standards has also strengthened my understanding of social dynamics and strategic business behaviour, particularly how institutions, governance arrangements, and market coordination shape industry outcomes."
